Payment Policy

Xpress Service bills you through an online billing account for purchases of services. You agree to pay Xpress Service all charges at the prices then in effect for the services you or other persons using your billing account may purchase, and you authorize Xpress Service to charge your chosen payment provider for any such purchases. You agree to make payment using Stripe. If you have ordered a service that is subject to recurring charges then you consent to our charging on a recurring basis, without requiring your prior approval from you for each recurring charge until such time as you cancel the applicable product or service. Xpress Service reserves the right to correct any errors or mistakes in pricing that it makes even if it has already requested or received payment. All payments shall be in U.S. dollars.

1. Xpress Service Fees

1.1 Fees for Independent Contractors

  • Service Fees

    Independent Contractors will pay Xpress Service a service fee for the use of the Site Services including the communication, invoicing, reporting, dispute resolution and payment services, including facilitating arbitration services and Hourly Payment Protection (the “Service Fees”).
    The Service Fees (to use the Site Services) are paid solely by Independent Contractor. When a Client pays an Independent Contractor for a Project or when funds related to a Project are otherwise released to a Independent Contractor as required by the applicable Escrow Instructions Xpress Service Escrow will credit the Independent Contractor Escrow Account for the full amount paid or released by the Client, and then subtract and disburse to Xpress Service the Service Fee.

  • Disbursement Fees

    Independent Contractors will pay Xpress Service a disbursement fee for remitting payments to their preferred payment method (“Disbursement Fee”). The Disbursement Fee is paid to Xpress Service in consideration of costs incurred and administration of disbursements via the disbursement method requested by Independent Contractor and varies by disbursement method.

1.2 Client/User Fees

Clients pay Xpress Service a fee for payment processing and administration related to the Independent Contractor Fees they pay to Independent Contractors they engage through the Site.

2. VAT and Other Taxes

Xpress Service may be required by applicable law to collect taxes or levies including, without limitation, withholding income tax or VAT in the state of the Independent Contractor (the "Taxes").

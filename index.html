Xpress Service

Xpress Service, is a platform which is committed to providing home owners and individual contractors with outstanding services that are home based such as plumbing, electrical, HVAC, carpet cleaning, maid service etc. All our service professionals operate on a simple but powerful principle - a job’s not done right.

We know that caring for your home can be stressful, so we want you to feel reassured knowing that we take great pride in the work we do, and we make sure it’s done to the highest standard. That’s why we’re proud to stand behind our work with the Xpress Service Done Right Promise. If it’s not done right, we promise to make it right.

Xpress Service Key Features

Xpress Service enables its users to enjoy the following features:-

Secure real-time booking system in place you choose as which allow the customer to book as per their schedule - the flows goes as follow :

  • Book a service, wait for the provider to accept or decline, either way both parties will be notified of any even related to them.
  • We have a secure payment system in place Strippe gateway – if the provider accept – upon acceptance – the customer can pay .
  • The location for service is pass down to the provider (contractor) – when the contractor is on its way a notification alert will be sent to the customer, as well as when the provider (contractor) arrive to the destination address for service.
  • The customer will be notify when the provider (contractor) start the job and finished – where the customer validate the job in the end – and also can review the contractor with rating and comments. .

In addition to that we regroup contractors under one app – you can order services with just a click of a button.
